Three ways to play

Flat-Rate Escalator

Pure, predictable competition.

Every round costs its round number in cents — Round 1 is a penny, Round 100 is a dollar. Everyone who reaches the end has paid exactly the same amount.

Streak Saver

Play for free if you're smart enough.

Answer correctly and the next round is free. Only a wrong answer costs you — getting a question wrong doesn't just hurt your score, it costs real money.

Milestone Booster

High-stakes game show energy.

Flat per-tier pricing from Bronze to Platinum, with guaranteed prize bonuses injected at Rounds 25, 50, and 75 as the field narrows.

How it works

1. Load tokens

Buy a token bundle to unlock rounds as you play — bigger bundles get bonus tokens.

2. Answer fast, answer right

Every question is timed. Correct answers score points; speed earns a bonus. Our servers keep time, not your phone.

3. Top 3 split the prize pool

60% of everyone's entry fees becomes the prize pool, split 50/30/20 between 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place at Round 100.
