Three ways to play

Same brain, different thrill. Pick your vibe.

📈

Flat-Rate Escalator

Pure, predictable competition.

Every round costs its round number in cents — Round 1 is a penny, Round 100 is a dollar. Everyone who reaches the end paid exactly the same.

🔥

Streak Saver

Play free if you're smart enough.

Answer correctly and the next round is free. Only a wrong answer costs you — get it wrong and it stings your score and your wallet.

🗺️

Milestone Booster: Treasure Hunt

Collect clues. Cash in big.

Same round-by-round pricing as the Escalator. Every 10th round is a clue worth an escalating multiplier — nail all 9 and Round 100 pays out the whole treasure in one shot. Miss the final answer, though, and the treasure's gone.

🔥 3 the hard way: answer three rounds in a row correctly and every correct answer after that credits the round's cost right back to your balance — a hot streak can carry a small deposit much further into the game than it could pay for alone.
