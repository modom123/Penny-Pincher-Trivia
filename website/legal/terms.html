3. The Token Wallet System

Each Token represents $0.01 USD of in-game value. Tokens are sold in bundles, and larger bundles include bonus Tokens: $1.00 = 100 Tokens, $5.00 = 600 Tokens, $10.00 = 1,300 Tokens, $20.00 = 2,800 Tokens. Tokens unlock trivia rounds and are non-refundable once a round is unlocked, win or lose.

4. Game Rules & Scoring

Each game is up to 100 progressively priced rounds. Scoring is determined solely by our servers — our server clock, not your device, is authoritative for all timing and cutoffs.

5. Prizes & Payouts

60% of the accumulated prize pool is distributed to the top 3 scorers (50% / 30% / 20%); the remainder is retained as a platform fee. Winnings may be subject to identity verification and tax reporting.

6. Ties & Sudden Death Overtime

Ties for a top-3 finish at Round 100 trigger a live tiebreaker under a shortened timer at a flat per-question fee, until the tie is resolved.
