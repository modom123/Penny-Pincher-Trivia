Game Modes & Prize Structure

Flat-Rate Escalator

Round N costs $0.01 × N (Round 1 = $0.01 … Round 100 = $1.00). Maximum possible spend per player: $50.50. Prize pool is 60% of aggregate entry fees; 40% is retained as a platform fee. Distribution: 1st place 50%, 2nd 30%, 3rd 20%.

Streak Saver

Same per-round pricing, but a correct answer makes the next round free; only an incorrect answer charges the round fee.

Milestone Booster

Flat per-tier pricing (Bronze through Platinum). At Rounds 25, 50, and 75, 5% of the then-current prize pool — funded entirely by entrant fees already collected, never by the sponsor — is paid immediately to whichever eligible entrant is leading at that moment (split evenly among ties). Each payout reduces the pool remaining for the Round 100 distribution by the same amount.

Sudden Death Overtime

Applies to all three modes: if two or more players are tied for a top-3 finish at Round 100, a live, shrinking-timer tiebreaker resolves it at a flat entry fee per question.
