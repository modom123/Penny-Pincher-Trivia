## What this package is

This package was prepared by the business **with AI assistance and without any
attorney's involvement**. It is research work-product intended to reduce the
hours your firm spends on first-pass research — not to substitute for your
analysis. Specifically:

- Every statute, regulation, case, and agency action cited herein is marked
  **[VERIFY]** and was compiled from general knowledge, not a current statutory
  or Shepard's check. Treat every citation as a lead, not an authority.
- Every "draft determination" is a hypothesis for you to confirm, revise, or
  reject. None of it has been relied upon: **no real-money play, marketing, or
  app-store submission has occurred or will occur before your written answers.**
- Where the earlier "Legal Clearance Intake Package v2" (PDF) conflicts with
  this package, **this package controls** — document 01 corrects two material
  misdescriptions in the PDF (payment rail; no-winner pool handling) found in a
  code audit on 2026-07-23.
